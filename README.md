# KPI Tracking Dashboard | Tableau

## Project Overview

An interactive Tableau dashboard designed to monitor business performance through key metrics such as Sales, Profit, Quantity, Revenue by State, Weekly Trends, Product Performance, and Fulfillment Analysis.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to track business health, identify growth opportunities, and make data-driven decisions through dynamic visual analytics.

---

## Business Objective

The objective of this dashboard is to provide a centralized reporting solution that allows business users to:

- Monitor Sales, Profit, and Quantity KPIs
- Compare performance against previous periods
- Analyze regional revenue performance
- Track weekly sales and profit trends
- Identify top-performing product categories
- Monitor SKU-level performance
- Evaluate fulfillment and courier status

---

## Dashboard Features

### Executive KPI Cards

- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Quantity
- Previous Year Comparison
- Dynamic Year Filter

### Regional Analysis

- Revenue by State
- State-wise Performance Ranking

### Product Analytics

- Top 5 Categories by Revenue
- Top 10 SKUs
- Product Performance Analysis

### Trend Analysis

- Weekly Sales Trends
- Weekly Profit Trends
- Year-over-Year Performance

### Operational Monitoring

- Fulfillment Status Analysis
- Courier Status Distribution
